Looking for a mentor?

Are you a person who wants to connect with a Mentor? Tibetan Scholarship Alumni Association is a strong network of TSP alumni across the world in equally diverse fields. We have Tibetan mentors that are supportive individuals who will offer guidance and encouragement to help set academic and/or career goals and taking steps to achieve them. Fill the form with your details and we will try our best to connect you to the mentor of your choice.
